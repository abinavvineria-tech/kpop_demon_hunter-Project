Plot Summary

KPop Demon Hunters follows the story of Huntrix, a K-pop girl group whose members lead double lives as demon hunters. They use their music to reinforce the Honmoon barrier that keeps demons from entering the human world.

When a rival boy band called the Saja Boys emerges, Huntrix discovers that the members are actually demons in disguise who seek to break the barrier and allow their demon king to enter the human realm. As the two groups clash in spectacular battles, secrets are revealed that will change everything.

Combining the vibrant world of K-pop with supernatural action, this animated musical explores themes of identity, friendship, and the power of music to unite and protect.
